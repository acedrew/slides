what is retro-commissioning?

process to restore and optimize operating building systems to their design intent

slide.notes

intent ≠ state

slide.notes

buildings are complicated.

conditioning spaces in commercial buildings more closely resembles a complex industrial process than your home AC

slide.notes

an industrial process with a different product in every room, and the product changes multiple times each day

slide.notes

focus on energy efficiency at the design level encourages adoption of complex systems to achieve efficiency goals

slide.notes

if you don’t see the parallels yet...

slide.notes

system designers rarely connect with system operators

facilities staff are operating systems without understanding the design intentions

slide.notes

rather than embracing and tackling the complexity head on, market has looked to vendors to hide it

slide.notes

Our goals

  • define the metrics that drive sustained operational excellence

  • reduce energy consumption and carbon impact of the built environment

  • raise the standard for comfort and reliability

  • develop the workforce that can operate and maintain the building of the future

  • deliver an aggressive ROI that allows investment in future innovations

slide.notes

deliver data and insights to public to ensure accountability and operational excellence is sustained
